## About

I am a Postdoctoral Associate in the [Department of Mathematical Sciences](https://www.cmu.edu/math/index.html) at Carnegie Mellon University. I received my Ph.D. in mathematics at the [University of Pennsylvania](https://www.math.upenn.edu/) in 2024 under the supervision of [Robert Strain](https://strain.math.upenn.edu/) and [Yoichiro Mori](https://www.sas.upenn.edu/~y1mori/). My primary research interest is the analytical study of well-posedness of PDE systems describing free boundary problems arising in fluid dynamics. I am also interested in applying boundary integral methods to design numerical schemes for accurate and robust simulation of such systems.
